ANNA CHORNIY

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Medical Social Sciences and Buehler Center for Health Policy and Economics at Northwestern University. I am also Faculty Associate in the Institute for Policy Research. My research focuses on Health economics and encompasses elements of Industrial Organization and Labor economics.

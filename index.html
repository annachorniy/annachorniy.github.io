ANNA CHORNIY

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Pediatrics and Center for Child Health Services Research at Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai. My research focuses on the relationship between health insurance and health, particularly in relation to Medicaid program enrollment, coverage, and financing rules. I examine how these factors affect children’s health, including those in foster care and those with complex needs.
